Mikael Blomkvist, a journalist disgraced by a libel conviction, is hired by Henrik Vanger, an aging industrialist, to solve the decades-old disappearance of his niece, Harriet. Alongside Lisbeth Salander, a brilliant but troubled hacker, Blomkvist delves into the Vanger family's dark history, uncovering a web of corruption and cruelty. Larsson's intricate plotting and compelling characters make for a gripping read that combines elements of mystery, thriller, and social commentary. "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" is a powerful exploration of violence against women and the quest for justice in a society rife with corruption. Its gripping narrative and unforgettable characters have captivated readers worldwide.
