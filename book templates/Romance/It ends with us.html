Lily, a resilient woman who hails from a quaint Maine town, has tirelessly pursued her dreams in bustling Boston. Her journey has been one of grit and determination, culminating in her own successful business venture. However, her world is turned upside down when she crosses paths with Ryle Kincaid, a captivating neurosurgeon. Despite his charm and undeniable attraction, Ryle harbors a deep aversion to commitment, leaving Lily grappling with doubts about their budding relationship. As she delves deeper into Ryle's past, she finds herself haunted by memories of Atlas Corrigan, her first love and a poignant reminder of the life she left behind. When Atlas unexpectedly resurfaces, Lily's newfound happiness with Ryle is thrown into jeopardy, forcing her to confront the complexities of love, loyalty, and the enduring ties that bind her to her past.
