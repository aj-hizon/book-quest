"My Mechanical Romance" follows Bel, a reluctant participant in her school's robotics club, who discovers her talent for engineering. Despite initial clashes with club captain Mateo Luna, they bond over late-night sessions building a combat-ready robot for Nationals. Alongside themes of first love and self-discovery, the novel delves into the challenges faced by girls of color in STEM fields.
