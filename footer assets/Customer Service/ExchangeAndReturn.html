Exchange and Return

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to exchange or return an item, please follow the guidelines below.

Return Policy

  • Eligibility:

    Items can be returned within 30 days of receipt.

  • Condition:

    Items must be in their original condition and packaging.

  • Process:

    Contact our customer service to initiate a return. We will provide you with a return authorization and instructions.

Exchange Policy

  • Eligibility:

    Exchanges are available for defective or damaged items.

  • Process:

    Contact us with details of the issue, and we will arrange for a replacement.

Refunds:

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. You will be notified via email once your refund is issued.
